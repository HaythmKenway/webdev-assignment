Karnan is an Indian Tamil-language action drama film directed by Mari Selvaraj, and produced by Kalaipuli S. Thanu under his V Creations banner. The film stars Dhanush, Lal, Yogi Babu, Natarajan Subramaniam, Rajisha Vijayan, Gouri G. Kishan and Lakshmi Priya Chandramouli. It marks the Tamil debut of Rajisha Vijayan
Release date: 9 April 2021 (India) Director: Mari Selvaraj Music director: Santhosh Narayanan Cinematography: Theni Eashwar Producer: Kalaippuli S Thanu
